Ali Pirsadeghi, MSc.

Master's in Biochemistry, AJUMS

Ahvaz, Iran

My research focuses on cancer, particularly glioblastoma, where I explore combination therapy and regenerative medicine approaches using 3D models. I aim to enhance treatment strategies and improve understanding of the disease. Additionally, I have experience working with Linux and R programming languages, specializing in RNA-seq and single-cell analysis to uncover insights into gene expression and cellular heterogeneity.
